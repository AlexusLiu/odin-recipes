Delicious sweet grilled steak that will have your friends and family begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 (6 ounce) boneless New York strip steaks
  • 3 cups bourbon whiskey
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar

Directions

step 1

Gently tenderize steaks with a meat mallet. With a sharp knife, lightly
score the meat on one side diagonally. Place steaks in a casserole dish,
scored side up, and pour bourbon over them. Rub the brown sugar evenly
over each steak. Marinate in refrigerator for 1 to 3 hours.

step 2

Preheat grill to high heat, and lightly oil grate.

step 3

Place the steaks on the hot grill, with the sugar side down. Let cook
until sugar has caramelized, 3 to 5 minutes, then flip steaks, and finish
cooking to desired doneness.
